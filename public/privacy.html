Privacy Policy

At MOONXURY, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you strictly provide to us when registering for tickets, including your name, email address, and phone number. Payment information is processed securely by Razorpay and is not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process ticket bookings, send confirmation emails and tickets, and communicate event updates. We may also use your email to inform you about future events by THEMOON.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data is shared only with our trusted partners (like Razorpay for payments) as necessary to provide our services.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to or correction of your personal data held by us. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.
