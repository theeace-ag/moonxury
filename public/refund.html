Cancellation & Refund Policy

Please review our cancellation and refund policy carefully before purchasing tickets.

1. Ticket Cancellation

Once purchased, tickets for MOONXURY are non-cancellable and non-refundable. However, they may be transferable with prior approval from the organizers.

2. Event Cancellation or Postponement

In the unlikely event that MOONXURY is cancelled by the organizers due to unforeseen circumstances, a full refund of the ticket face value will be initiated to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

If the event is postponed, your ticket will be valid for the new date.

3. Refund Process

For eligible refunds (event cancellation only), the amount will be credited back to the source account (card/bank/UPI) used during the transaction.

4. Contact

For any disputes or issues regarding refunds, please contact us at the details provided on our Contact Us page.
