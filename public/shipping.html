Shipping & Delivery Policy

For MOONXURY event tickets, we operate a digital delivery system.

1. Digital Delivery

Upon successful payment confirmation, tickets are generated instantly and delivered electronically.

  • Email: A copy of your ticket with a QR code will be sent to the email address provided during registration.
  • Download: You can download your ticket immediately from the confirmation page on our website.

2. Delivery Timelines

Digital tickets are typically delivered within minutes of payment success. In rare cases of network delays, please allow up to 30 minutes. If you do not receive your ticket, please contact our support team.

3. Physical Tickets

There are no physical tickets shipped for this event. Entry is granted solely based on the digital ticket/QR code.
